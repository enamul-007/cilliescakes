Our Story

A Family Passion For Baking

Cillie's is a family-owned business which started with my mother Shireen Mehta in Karachi, Pakistan. After graduating from Hotel School in Switzerland, I began my American journey in Connecticut in 1990 — and the dream of sharing our cakes with the world came with me.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

1
The Beginning · Karachi, Pakistan
A Mother's Recipe

My mother Shireen Mehta started Cillie's in Karachi, Pakistan — baking whipped cream cakes with love, using recipes passed down through generations.

2
1990 · Connecticut, USA
A New Chapter in America

After graduating from Hotel School in Switzerland, I moved to Connecticut to start my American life. The love of baking travelled with me across the ocean.

3
Moving to Houston, TX
Houston Becomes Home

I moved to Houston and worked in several hotels before following my true calling — honouring my mother's legacy by opening Cillie's Cakes on Wilcrest Drive.

4
20 Years Later · Today
Our Current Home

After 20 successful years, we opened our current location at 867 Dulles Avenue, Suite A, Stafford TX. Same passion, same recipes — bigger kitchen.
